Hangover Rescue

Rapid relief, delivered drop by drop.

Woken up with a pounding headache, nausea and that heavy, foggy fatigue? Our Hangover Rescue drip is built to turn the morning around. A tailored blend of hydrating fluids, electrolytes and replenishing vitamins is delivered straight into the bloodstream for full absorption — helping you rehydrate quickly, ease the worst of the symptoms and feel like yourself again far sooner than fluids and painkillers alone.

Benefits

  • Rehydrate rapidly and restore lost electrolytes for fast relief
  • Ease nausea, headache and sensitivity so you can function again
  • Replenish the B vitamins and nutrients depleted by a heavy night
  • Support your body's natural detox processes and lift brain fog

What's Inside

  • Saline & ElectrolytesReplenishes lost fluids directly into the bloodstream for rapid rehydration
  • Vitamin B-ComplexSupports alcohol metabolism and restores nutrients depleted overnight
  • MagnesiumHelps relax tight muscles and ease tension headaches
  • Anti-Sickness SupportSettles nausea so you can keep fluids down (added at the practitioner's discretion)
  • Anti-Inflammatory SupportTargets stubborn head pain where appropriate
  • GlutathioneAntioxidant support to help clear the by-products of a heavy night

How It Works

  • Bypasses the stomach — fluids and nutrients go straight into the bloodstream, ideal when nothing will stay down
  • Settles the stomach first — anti-sickness support, where used, helps break the cycle of nausea and dehydration
  • Speeds up recovery — rehydrates the whole system in well under an hour rather than over a long, slow morning
  • Clears the fog — B vitamins and electrolytes help restore mental clarity and steadiness

Before & Aftercare

Before Your Treatment

  • Have a little water before your appointment if you can keep it down
  • Disclose any medications, allergies or medical conditions at consultation
  • Let us know how much you've had to drink and when — it helps us treat you safely
  • Wear loose, comfortable clothing with easy access to your arm

✓ Aftercare — Do

  • Keep sipping water through the rest of the day to stay topped up
  • Eat a light, nourishing meal once your appetite returns
  • Rest — your body is still recovering, so take it easy
  • Note that most people feel relief quickly, with steady improvement over the day

✕ Aftercare — Avoid

  • More alcohol for the rest of the day
  • Strenuous exercise straight afterwards — gentle movement is fine
  • Removing the small plaster from the injection site for an hour
  • Skipping meals — food supports your recovery

When to Seek Medical Care Instead

A rescue drip supports recovery from a normal hangover — not a medical emergency. Please seek urgent in-person care if you experience chest pain, confusion, an inability to stay awake, seizures, vomiting blood, severe tremors or shaking, or slow breathing, as these can be signs of alcohol poisoning or another serious condition. If you find yourself needing frequent recovery drips, it's worth speaking with your GP about your overall health and drinking patterns.
