PRP & Vampire Treatments

Your body's own healing power, harnessed.

Platelet-Rich Plasma (PRP) therapy uses your own blood, processed to concentrate the platelets and growth factors, then reintroduced to areas needing repair. It's a natural, regenerative approach that triggers your body's own healing — for skin, hair and beyond.

  • Stimulate hair regrowth and thicken thinning areas
  • Improve skin tone, texture, scarring and elasticity over time
  • Cumulative, long-lasting results that work with your body, not against it

Before & Aftercare

Before Your Treatment

  • Eat a balanced meal and arrive well hydrated — a blood draw is required
  • Avoid alcohol and blood-thinning medications for 48 hours before
  • Disclose any blood disorders, autoimmune conditions or medications
  • For hair treatments, wash your hair the morning of the appointment

✓ Aftercare — Do

  • Apply a cool compress to reduce any redness or mild swelling
  • Stay well hydrated to support your body's healing response
  • Sleep on a clean pillowcase and avoid touching your face
  • Use SPF 30+ daily for the next two weeks

✕ Aftercare — Avoid

  • Make-up for 24 hours after a facial treatment
  • Active skincare ingredients (retinol, acids) for one week
  • Strenuous exercise, saunas and steam rooms for 48 hours
  • Direct sun exposure and other facial treatments for two weeks

What to Expect

Redness and mild swelling are normal for 24–48 hours after treatment. Initial improvements appear within 2–3 weeks, with full results developing over 3 months as collagen builds. A course of 3 sessions is typically recommended for optimal results.
