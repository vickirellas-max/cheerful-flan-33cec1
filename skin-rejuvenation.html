Before & Aftercare

Before Your Treatment

  • Avoid active skincare (retinol, AHA, BHA) for one week before
  • Inform us of any cold sores, current breakouts or skin conditions
  • Avoid sun exposure and tanning for two weeks before treatment
  • Arrive with clean skin, no make-up and well hydrated

✓ Aftercare — Do

  • Apply a calming, fragrance-free moisturiser for the first few days
  • Use SPF 30+ daily — your skin will be highly sun-sensitive
  • Sleep on a clean pillowcase and keep your hands away from your face
  • Drink plenty of water to support skin recovery

✕ Aftercare — Avoid

  • Make-up for 24 hours after the treatment
  • Active ingredients (retinol, acids, vitamin C) for 5–7 days
  • Strenuous exercise, sweating, saunas and steam rooms for 48 hours
  • Direct sun, tanning beds and other facial treatments for two weeks

What to Expect

Mild redness and a sensation of warmth are normal for 24–48 hours. Skin may feel slightly rough as it renews, with full results appearing over 4–6 weeks as fresh collagen develops.
