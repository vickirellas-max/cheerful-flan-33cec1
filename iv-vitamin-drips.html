What is IV Vitamin Drip Therapy?

IV infusion therapy entails the direct administration of a tailored mixture of vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants into your veins. This technique avoids the limitations of the digestive system, achieving 100% bioavailability of the nutrients. The outcome is a powerful infusion of vitamins that offers quick and enduring benefits.

Before & Aftercare

Before Your Treatment

  • Eat a balanced meal 1–2 hours before to avoid lightheadedness
  • Arrive well hydrated — drink at least 500ml of water beforehand
  • Disclose any allergies, medications or medical conditions
  • Wear loose, comfortable clothing with easy arm access

✓ Aftercare — Do

  • Continue drinking water throughout the rest of the day
  • Eat a nourishing meal afterwards if your appetite returns quickly
  • Rest if you feel tired — your body is absorbing intensively
  • Note that some clients feel benefits immediately; others over 24–48 hours

✕ Aftercare — Avoid

  • Alcohol for the rest of the day after your drip
  • Strenuous exercise immediately afterwards — light walking is fine
  • Removing the small plaster from the injection site for 1 hour
  • Skipping meals — nutrition supports the absorption process

What to Expect

Most drips take 30–60 minutes. You may feel a cool sensation as the drip flows, and a vitamin-like taste in your mouth. Benefits range from immediate (hydration, energy) to cumulative (NAD+, glow), with regular sessions giving the best results.
