Biorevitalisation & Profhilo

Hydration that works from the inside out.

Biorevitalisation treatments deliver hyaluronic acid, vitamins, amino acids and antioxidants directly into the upper skin layers — restoring deep hydration, boosting elasticity and improving overall skin quality. Unlike fillers, these treatments don't add volume; they reawaken the skin's own ability to regenerate.

Benefits

  • Deeply hydrate from within for a plump, dewy, healthy-looking complexion
  • Stimulate collagen and elastin production for firmer, more elastic skin
  • Improve overall skin texture, tone and luminosity
  • Reduce the appearance of fine lines, especially around the eyes and mouth

Before & Aftercare

Before Your Treatment

  • Avoid alcohol and blood-thinning medications for 24 hours before
  • Disclose any recent skincare procedures, allergies or skin conditions
  • Arrive with clean skin and no make-up where possible
  • Eat a small meal beforehand to minimise dizziness

✓ Aftercare — Do

  • Stay well hydrated for several days following your treatment
  • Gently cleanse and moisturise with a mild, fragrance-free product
  • Use SPF 30 or higher daily — your skin will be more photo-sensitive
  • Sleep on your back the first night to avoid putting pressure on the treated area

✕ Aftercare — Avoid

  • Touching, pressing or massaging the small injection bumps for 24 hours
  • Make-up for 12 hours and active skincare ingredients (retinol, acids) for 5 days
  • Saunas, steam rooms, hot yoga and intense exercise for 48 hours
  • Direct sun exposure, tanning beds and other facial treatments for two weeks

What to Expect

Small injection bumps are normal for the first 12 hours and settle naturally. Most clients see visible glow within a week, with full results developing over a course of 2–3 sessions spaced 4 weeks apart.
