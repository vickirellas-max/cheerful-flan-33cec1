Fat Dissolving

Targeted contouring, without surgery.

Fat dissolving injections target stubborn pockets of fat that resist diet and exercise, gently breaking down fat cells which are then naturally cleared by the body. It's a non-surgical way to refine and sculpt — from a softer jawline to a smoother stomach.

Benefits

  • Permanently destroy targeted fat cells in stubborn problem areas
  • Refine the jawline and reduce the appearance of a double chin
  • Smooth and contour bra fat, arms, stomach, thighs and love handles
  • Non-surgical with minimal downtime — return to daily life the same day

Before & Aftercare

Before Your Treatment

  • Avoid alcohol and blood-thinning medications for 24 hours before
  • Maintain a balanced diet and good hydration in the days leading up
  • Wear loose, comfortable clothing to the appointment
  • Inform us of any allergies, recent surgeries or current medications

✓ Aftercare — Do

  • Drink at least 2L of water daily to help your body process the fat
  • Walk and stay gently active to support lymphatic drainage
  • Wear loose clothing for the first 48 hours to allow for swelling
  • Continue a healthy diet to support and prolong your results

✕ Aftercare — Avoid

  • Strenuous exercise, saunas and steam rooms for 72 hours
  • Alcohol for at least 48 hours after treatment
  • Tight clothing pressing on the treated area for 48 hours
  • Massage or pressure on the area for one week

What to Expect

Swelling, redness, tenderness and bruising are normal and can last up to two weeks — this is a sign the treatment is working. Visible results appear after 4–6 weeks, with a course of 3–5 sessions typically recommended.
