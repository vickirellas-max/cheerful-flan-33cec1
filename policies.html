Booking & Treatment Policies

Clear terms, full transparency.

Please review our booking policies and treatment conditions before your appointment. By booking with us, you confirm that you have read, understood and agreed to all terms below.

Booking Policies & Conditions

To ensure smooth scheduling and fairness to all clients, please carefully review the policies below.

01 Appointment Booking +

Appointments must be booked in advance via our online system, phone, or in person.

A booking is not confirmed until a deposit has been received.

We may request basic personal and medical information for first-time clients to ensure safe and appropriate treatment, especially where relevant medical history applies.

02 Deposit Policy +
  • A £50 deposit is required for all minor treatments.
  • A £100 deposit is required for BBL and breast filler procedures.
  • All deposits are strictly non-refundable but may be applied toward your treatment if all policies are followed.
03 Late Arrival Policy +

Clients are expected to arrive on time for their appointment. A grace period of 15 minutes is allowed.

If you arrive more than 15 minutes late, your appointment will be cancelled, your deposit will be forfeited, and no rescheduling will be offered.

04 No-Show Policy +

Failure to attend your appointment without prior notice will result in:

  • Full forfeiture of your deposit
  • No option for rescheduling

A new deposit will be required for any future bookings.

05 Rescheduling Policy +

Appointments may be rescheduled up to 48 hours before the scheduled time. This allows us enough time to offer your slot to another client.

Requests made within 48 hours of the appointment will not be accepted, and the deposit will be forfeited.

06 Cancellation Policy +

Cancellations must be made at least 48 hours in advance. Late cancellations (within 48 hours) will result in loss of deposit.

07 Groupon Bookings +

Groupon clients are given a maximum of 15 minutes grace period.

If you arrive 16 minutes late or more, or fail to attend, your voucher will be marked as redeemed and rescheduling will not be permitted.

08 Complimentary Top-Up +

Cancellation or rescheduling of a complimentary top-up appointment is not allowed.

Failure to attend or attempting to reschedule will result in the loss of the complimentary top-up, and it will no longer be offered free of charge.

09 Exceptions +

In genuine emergencies (e.g. serious illness, accidents, or bereavement), exceptions may be considered at management discretion. Supporting evidence may be required.

10 Refund Policy +

Deposits are non-refundable unless the appointment is cancelled by the clinic.

No refunds are given for change of mind, failure to follow aftercare, or unused services.

11 Agreement +

By booking an appointment, you confirm that you have read, understood, and agreed to these terms and conditions.

Failure to read or acknowledge these policies does not exempt you from complying with them.

Treatment Conditions & Compliance

For safety, transparency and regulatory compliance, please review the following carefully.

01 Unsafe or Unsuitable Treatments +

We reserve the right to refuse or decline treatment if it is deemed unsafe, medically inappropriate, or not in the client's best interest.

This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Contraindications based on medical history
  • Unrealistic expectations
  • Conditions that may compromise safe results

In such cases, a treatment may be postponed or cancelled for safety reasons.

02 Client Responsibility & Medical Disclosure +

Clients are required to provide accurate, complete, and honest medical information prior to any treatment.

Failure to disclose relevant medical conditions, medications, allergies, or previous procedures may result in:

  • Complications or unsatisfactory results
  • Voiding of liability on the clinic's part

We are not responsible for any issues arising from incorrect, incomplete, or withheld medical information.

03 Aftercare Responsibility +

Clients must strictly follow all aftercare instructions provided by the practitioner.

Failure to follow aftercare advice may lead to:

  • Poor or compromised results
  • Increased risk of complications

The clinic will not be held responsible for outcomes affected by non-compliance with aftercare guidelines.

04 Results & Complaints +

Aesthetic treatments are not an exact science, and results may vary between individuals.

We do not guarantee specific outcomes, as results depend on factors such as:

  • Individual healing response
  • Lifestyle and aftercare compliance

Any concerns or complaints must be reported within a reasonable timeframe following treatment. We will assess all concerns professionally and may offer a review appointment where appropriate.

Refunds are not provided based on dissatisfaction alone where the treatment has been performed correctly and safely.

05 Complications & Follow-Up +

In the rare event of complications, clients must contact the clinic immediately for guidance.

Seeking external treatment without notifying us may affect our ability to assess responsibility.

06 Data Protection & GDPR +

All personal and medical information is handled in accordance with GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) and applicable data protection laws.

Your data will be:

  • Collected only for legitimate medical and administrative purposes
  • Stored securely and confidentially
  • Not shared with third parties without your consent, unless required by law

Clients have the right to:

  • Request access to their personal data
  • Request corrections or updates
  • Request deletion where legally applicable
07 Consent & Legal Compliance +

All clients are required to complete and sign a consultation and consent form prior to treatment.

By proceeding with any treatment, you confirm that:

  • You understand the risks, benefits, and limitations
  • You have provided accurate information
  • You agree to comply with all aftercare instructions and clinic policies
08 Policy Enforcement +

These conditions are in place to protect both the client and the clinic.

Failure to comply with these terms may result in refusal of treatment, cancellation of services, or restriction from future bookings.

By proceeding with treatment, you acknowledge and accept all of the terms outlined above.
