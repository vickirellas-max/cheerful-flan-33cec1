Before & Aftercare

Before Your Treatment

  • Disclose all previous filler treatments — type, brand and dates if possible
  • Inform us of any allergies, particularly to bee venom
  • A patch test is required 24–48 hours before for first-time clients
  • Avoid alcohol and blood-thinning medications for 24 hours before

✓ Aftercare — Do

  • Apply a cold compress gently to reduce swelling in the first 24 hours
  • Stay hydrated and rest where possible after treatment
  • Sleep with your head slightly elevated the first night
  • Wait at least two weeks before considering any new filler in the same area

✕ Aftercare — Avoid

  • Touching, pressing or massaging the treated area for 24 hours
  • Strenuous exercise, saunas and steam rooms for 48 hours
  • Alcohol for at least 24 hours, as it may worsen swelling
  • Make-up over the injection sites for 12 hours

What to Expect

Swelling, redness and tenderness are normal for 3–7 days and may temporarily look more pronounced than the original filler. Final results — your natural facial contours — appear after 2 weeks once swelling fully settles.
