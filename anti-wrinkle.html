Anti-Wrinkle

Smooth lines today. Prevent them tomorrow.

Anti-wrinkle injections relax the small muscles that cause expression lines, softening existing wrinkles and helping to prevent new ones from forming. The result is a refreshed, well-rested appearance — never frozen, always you.

Benefits

  • Soften forehead lines, frown lines (glabella) and crow's feet for a refreshed look
  • Prevent dynamic wrinkles from deepening into permanent static lines
  • Slim and contour the lower face with masseter treatment for a softer jawline
  • Reduce excessive sweating in the underarms, palms or feet (hyperhidrosis)

What's Included

  • One AreaTreats one expression zone — forehead, frown lines or crow's feet
  • Two AreasMost popular — combines two zones for balanced softening
  • Three AreasFull upper face treatment for a complete refresh

Before & Aftercare

Before Your Treatment

  • Avoid alcohol and blood-thinning medications (aspirin, ibuprofen) for 24 hours before
  • Disclose any history of allergies, neurological conditions or recent vaccinations
  • Inform us of any current medications, especially antibiotics
  • Arrive with clean skin, no make-up where possible, and well hydrated

✓ Aftercare — Do

  • Stay upright for at least four hours after your treatment
  • Gently exercise the treated muscles by frowning and smiling for the first hour
  • Stay hydrated and continue your normal skincare routine
  • Allow up to 14 days for the full effect to settle in

✕ Aftercare — Avoid

  • Lying flat, bending forward or massaging the treated area for 24 hours
  • Strenuous exercise, saunas, steam rooms and hot showers for 24 hours
  • Alcohol and blood-thinning medications for 24 hours after
  • Facials, laser treatments and microdermabrasion for two weeks

What to Expect

You may notice mild redness or small bumps for a few hours after treatment. Results begin appearing within 3–5 days and reach their full effect by 14 days, lasting 3–4 months on average.
